WHO WE ARE

FOCUSED.

FAST.

VERTICAL.

Kite Lane Group is an AI-native software studio building vertical SaaS products for high-stakes professional workflows. We operate at the intersection of finance, legal, and deal-making — industries where precision, speed, and trust are non-negotiable.

Our products are purpose-built, not retrofitted with AI. Each one is designed from the ground up around large language models, structured data, and the specific needs of practitioners who don't have time for general-purpose tools.

We are a lean, founder-led studio. Every product we ship reflects deep domain knowledge and a relentless focus on user outcomes over feature counts.
